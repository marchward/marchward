# Our licensing commitment

## The promise

**The Marchward engine and SDK are, and will remain, open source under the Apache License 2.0.** We will not relicense them under a source-available or proprietary license (no BSL, SSPL, or similar), and we will not pull functionality out of the open engine to force upgrades.

Marchward is open-core. The engine and SDK are open and stay open. The hosted control plane (managed credential vault, audit-as-a-service, multi-tenant access control, and approval workflows) is the commercial product. See `OPEN-VS-COMMERCIAL.md` for the boundary.

## What this means for you
- **Build on it.** You can self-host the engine, depend on it, and ship it inside your own products under Apache-2.0, indefinitely.
- **Contribute to it.** Improvements to the open engine stay open and benefit everyone, including us, since we ship the same engine.
- **No rug-pull.** If we ever changed the engine's license in a more restrictive direction, that would break this commitment publicly and on the record.
