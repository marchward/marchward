# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

**Please do not open a public issue for security problems.** Report privately to **security@marchward.ai**. If you need encryption, request our PGP key at that address.

Include, as best you can: the affected component and version, a description, reproduction steps or a proof of concept, and the impact you foresee.

## What to expect
- **Acknowledgement** within 2 business days.
- An initial assessment and severity triage within 5 business days.
- Coordinated disclosure: we will agree a timeline with you, fix, and credit you (if you wish) in the release notes. We aim to resolve high-severity issues within 30 days.

## Scope
In scope: the open **engine** and **SDK** in this repository. The hosted control plane (app.marchward.ai and api.marchward.ai) is also covered; report it the same way.

Out of scope: vulnerabilities in third-party dependencies (report upstream, but do tell us so we can pin or patch), and findings that require physical access or a compromised developer machine.

## Safe harbor
We will not pursue or support legal action against researchers who act in good faith, follow this policy, avoid privacy violations and service disruption, and give us reasonable time to remediate before disclosure.
