# Project Governance

## Model: single-vendor, stewarded by Marchward

The Marchward engine and SDK are an open-source project stewarded by Marchward, the company. We maintain the roadmap, review and merge contributions, cut releases, and make final technical decisions.

In practice:
- **Anyone can contribute** (see `CONTRIBUTING.md`); good contributions are welcome and credited.
- **Maintainers** are currently Marchward employees. We will add external maintainers as trusted contributors emerge, based on sustained, high-quality contribution.
- **Direction:** we own the roadmap and develop it in the open, through issues and discussions.

## Our commitments to the community
- The engine and SDK stay open. See `LICENSING-COMMITMENT.md` (no relicensing rug-pull).
- The open and paid boundary is stated up front. See `OPEN-VS-COMMERCIAL.md`.
- Security and conduct are handled transparently. See `SECURITY.md` and `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`.

## Future governance
As the project matures, we may move toward more neutral governance, such as a foundation, if and when that better serves the community. That is a future decision, not a launch commitment.

## How to get involved
Open an issue, start a discussion, or pick up a `good-first-issue`. Sustained contributors earn review responsibility, then maintainership.
