# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its Code of Conduct.

In short: we are committed to a harassment-free, welcoming, and respectful community for everyone, regardless of background or identity. Be kind, assume good faith, and disagree professionally.

**Reporting:** report unacceptable behavior to **team@marchward.ai**. Reports are handled confidentially.

The full standards, scope, and enforcement guidelines are those of the Contributor Covenant v2.1, available at the link above and incorporated here by reference.
